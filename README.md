Project Overview

This project analyzes the Tamil Nadu Assembly Election results from 2021 and 2026 to identify major political shifts, constituency flips, regional trends, and electoral competitiveness.

The analysis focuses on three research questions:

1. Geographic Story
2. Flip Story
3. Margin of Victory Story


Objective
The goal of this project is to understand how the political landscape changed between 2021 and 2026 and identify the major patterns behind constituency-level shifts.

Research Questions Answered

1. Geographic Story
How did political shifts vary across Tamil Nadu's regions?

2. Flip Story
How many constituencies changed winning parties between 2021 and 2026?

3. Margin of Victory Story
Which constituencies had the closest races in 2026?

Key Findings

1. 144 constituencies changed winning parties between 2021 and 2026.
2. The largest party-to-party shift was DMK → TVK, accounting for 57 flipped constituencies.
3. Chennai Metro recorded the highest concentration of constituency flips.
4. Several constituencies experienced highly competitive races with narrow victory margins.
